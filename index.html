🌿 Our Story

About AvoIndia

A decade of building India's most trusted avocado supply chain — from farm to fork.

🌍

India's Dedicated Avocado Import & Distribution Company

Founded in 2014, AvoIndia started with a simple mission: bring the world's finest avocados to India's growing food culture. Today, we are India's largest dedicated avocado importer and distributor, serving over 1,200 businesses across 28 states.

We work directly with certified farms in Mexico, Israel, Kenya, and Peru — ensuring traceability, quality, and fair prices at every step of the supply chain.

  • ✅ FSSAI Licensed Importer & Distributor
  • ✅ Direct farm partnerships — no middlemen
  • ✅ ISO 22000 certified cold-chain operations
  • ✅ Pan-India delivery with 48-hour freshness guarantee
  • ✅ Transparent, daily-updated market pricing
